About Us

Sports play a important role in everyone's busy life especially for students. Being involved in sports activities benefits a person in many ways. It does not provide only physical strength however it increases mental power too. Sports are very beneficial to us as they teach us punctuality, patient, discipline, teamwork and dedication. Playing sports help us in building and improving confidence level. If we practice sports on regular basis, we can be more active and healthy.
Our college's cultural club conducts a mega event with the cooperation and support of the respected teachers and honarable Principal Pangavhane Sir. This mega event name is COMBAT. COMBAT is the Annual sports event in GCOEARA, where enthusiastic students participate and show their talent in various sports as part of their respective teams.
It's an Intra-college sport event where various sport events are organized. Students of all departments compete with each other to secure General Trophy. General Trophy is awarded to the branch scoring maximum points in point table which is prepared by assigning predefined points to winners and runners-up of various sports events.
Every team event in the tournament will have one winner and one runner up. So for this we have points system for Department Trophy.

1. Date Announcement and Scheduling -

After proper planning and coordination date sheet of General championship was announced along with other activities . Various details like regular academic, timing of girls and boys hostel as well as upcoming examinations was seen. A very good care was taken to reduce the disturbance to the regular academic schedule.
Preliminary matches were scheduled from 18th March 2023. In between 18th to 31st March all the preliminary matches were scheduled. General championship match was scheduled on 11th to 12th April 2023. Semi final were scheduled on 11th April and finals were scheduled on 12th April.

2. Team formation and practice -

In the first two weeks of March 2023 selection process was conducted. Trials was taken by sports secretary, class sports secretary. Students shows great excitement to the selection and the participants in large numbers.
Management team designed a well coordinated schedule. Proper management of time spent on ground and instruments was done. Daily practices were conducted by players from all branches. Without no disturbance to academic activities the time from early morning to evening was well utilized.

3. Preliminary Matches -

Preliminary matches were conducted in between 18th and 31st March. Both boys and girls from all branches show a great interest in participation of indoor games likes carrom, chess, table tennis. And outdoor games like cricket and football for boys, volleyball, kho-kho, badminton and kabaddi. Also box cricket only for girls. All players gave their best to reach to the semi final.
Due to in semester examination, all activities related to general championship was temporarily stop upto 8th April after preliminary matches.

4. Semi Finals -

Semi final matches were conducted on 11th April 2023. All teams were completely charged up by the qualifications. Practice sessions were resumed for the preparation of semi final matches.

5. Finals -

The air in the campus was thick with excitement for the final matches. The qualified teams were all charged up and ready to compete in the final matches, held on 12th April 2023.
